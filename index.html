ELBOW REFLEX TEST
NOTE - In order to start the indentification, move your left elbow into the camera's view to play Spongebob Theme. To play Mozart Drill, move your right elbow into the camera's view.
Keep a small distance away from the camera, so your elbows are detected easily.

Once your elbow is detected, try to remove it from the camera's view as fast as possible. If the music plays once, you have succesfully completed the reflex test.
If it plays multiple times, you have lost. Play as many times until you succesfully complete the reflex test, or just for fun and enjoyment.
